about me

I am a curious creative that loves refining my skills to solve messy human problems and make cool things.

I have been a designer, a technical problem solver, a business administrator, a big picture planner. But every career I explored failed to allow me to utilize all of my skills. Until I found programming!

I quickly came to realize that programming is the ultimate weapon for both artists and engineers to create anything they can dream of, which makes it the perfect fit for me. Code can be as beautifully elegant as art, benefits from every tool in my toolkit, and inspires as much passion for the craft as any traditional artistic discipline.

I started out teaching myself and then dove into a four-month full-stack development program to learn even more with their support. I am excited to find new things to learn and make every day, and honored to be a part of this profession!
